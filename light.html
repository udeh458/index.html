Creamy Garlic Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 cup cream
  • Salt and pepper
  • Grated cheese

Steps:

  1. Boil pasta until tender.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and saut√© garlic.
  3. Add cream and let it simmer.
  4. Mix in the cooked pasta and season well.
  5. Top with cheese and serve warm.
